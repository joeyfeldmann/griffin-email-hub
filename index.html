How to copy from MCAE:
1. Open the sent email report in MCAE (the page with Delivery Rate, Open Rate, etc.)
2. Click anywhere in the stats area (not the top nav)
3. Press Ctrl+A then Ctrl+C to copy all visible text
4. Paste below and click Parse & import

Note on the bookmarklet

The bookmarklet works for copying data to clipboard, but Salesforce Lightning Experience embeds the Pardot report inside a cross-origin iframe — a browser security restriction that prevents the bookmarklet from reading the stats directly.

Recommended workflow: Use the Paste & parse tab (Ctrl+A → Ctrl+C on the report page → paste here). This reliably captures all stats and link click rows.
